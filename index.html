Welcome to ngMount Demo Page

In this demo we are using Owl Carousel as a jQuery plugin example.

How you would use the plugin in a non-Angular application

Your HTML: 
    <div id="owl-example" class="owl-carousel">
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      <div> Your Content </div>div
      ...
    </div>
.. and in your js: 
$(document).ready(function() {

  $("#owl-example").owlCarousel();

});

How you use the plugin in your Angular application

Your HTML: 
    <div ng-controller="myCtrl" class="owl-carousel" ng-mount="{plugin: 'owlCarousel', params:{items:4}}">
        <div ng-repeat="c in contents"> {{c}} </div>
    </div>
.. and in your js: 
    app.controller("myCtrl", function($scope) {
        $scope.contents = ["Content 1", "Content 2", "Content 3", "Content 4", "Content 5", "Content 6"]
    });
